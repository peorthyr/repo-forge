# Scan Workflow Stages

## Stage 0 - Intake
Collect target path, depth, and objective.

## Stage 1 - Inventory
Find architecture-governance files and classify them by role and quality.

## Stage 2 - Compatibility Assessment
Compare findings with canonical repository patterns.

## Stage 3 - Decision
Recommend keeper, seed, or no structural intervention.

## Stage 4 - Pattern Candidate Detection
Identify potentially better patterns and log assimilation candidates.

## Stage 5 - Handoff
Produce report and handoff package for keeper or seed.
